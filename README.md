# Adventure Works Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional profitability using the Adventure Works dataset. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.

## Business Questions (KPIs)

* What is the Total Sales Revenue generated?
* How many Orders were placed?
* Which Products and Categories generate the highest sales?
* Who are the Top Customers by Revenue?
* Which Sales Territories contribute the most revenue?
* What are the Monthly and Yearly Sales Trends?
* What is the Profit and Profit Margin percentage?
* How does Customer Purchasing Behavior vary across regions?

## Process

* Imported and combined FactInternetSales and Fact_Internet_Sales_New datasets.
* Performed data cleaning, transformation, and validation using Power Query.
* Built a star schema data model by connecting Fact and Dimension tables.
* Created calculated columns and DAX measures for business KPIs.
* Designed interactive dashboards with slicers, filters, and drill-through functionality.
* Developed visual reports for Sales, Customers, Products, and Regional Performance.

## Dashboard Features

* Executive Sales Dashboard
* Customer Analysis Dashboard
* Product Performance Dashboard
* Territory & Regional Analysis
* Interactive Filters and Slicers
* KPI Cards and Trend Analysis
* Dynamic Drill-Through Reports

## Project Insights

* Identified top-performing products and categories contributing the highest revenue.
* Analyzed customer purchasing patterns and high-value customers.
* Evaluated sales performance across different regions and territories.
* Tracked monthly and yearly sales growth trends.
* Measured profitability through Profit and Profit Margin analysis.

## Final Conclusion

The Adventure Works Sales Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by combining sales, customer, product, and regional insights. The analysis enables management to identify growth opportunities, improve customer targeting, optimize product strategies, and make informed business decisions based on real-time performance metrics.
